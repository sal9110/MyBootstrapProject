Rooms:

The approx. 18-22m² “standard class” rooms are located at the front of the seashore and boast the highest possible level of living and sleeping comfort. All standard rooms are equipped with a comfortable sleeping system, air conditioning, a cosy seating area, a desk, a flat screen TV, and a modern bathroom with shower.
